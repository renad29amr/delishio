Cheese Omelette


Ingredients

  1. 2 large eggs
  2. 2 tablespoons milk
  3. Salt and pepper, to taste
  4. 1 tablespoon butter
  5. 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook for 1â€“2 minutes until slightly set.
  4. Sprinkle cheese on one side of the omelette.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute until cheese melts.
