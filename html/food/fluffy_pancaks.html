Fluffy Pancakes Recipe


Ingredients

  1. cup all-purpose flour
  2. 2 tablespoons sugar
  3. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  4. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 cup milk
  6. 1 large egg
  7. 2 tablespoons melted butter
  8. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
