Frensh Toast


Ingredients

  1. 4 slices of bread
  2. 2 large eggs
  3. 1/2 cup milk
  4. 1 tablespoon sugar
  5. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  6. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Butter for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook the bread slices for 2â€“3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or powdered sugar
